B. V. R. Mohan Reddy

Ex Chairman NASSCOM

BVR Mohan Reddy (born 1950) is an Indian business executive and engineering manager who founded and served as Executive Chairman of Cyient, (formerly Infotech Enterprises). He joined the DCM Group in 1974, and has subsequently worked with MICO Bosch, HCL, and OMC Computers Limited. He was the Chairman of NASSCOM from 2014–2015, and is a prolific business analyst

Aloke Bajpai

Founder IXIGO

Aloke Bajpai is ixigo.com’s Co-Founder and CEO. Aloke graduated from IIT Kanpur in 2001 and started his career at Amadeus in Europe where he held key product and technology roles, building and managing large scale web-based products and networks for the first four years of his career. He then went to INSEAD for his MBA, and returned to India to launch ixigo.com in 2007. Under Aloke’s leadership over the last 7 years, ixigo has gone from a bootstrapped startup to India’s leading travel search and planning business, with over 20 million active users every month across mobile and web. Aloke is a TiE charter member and a mentor and advisor with several startups and accelerators.

Farid Ahsan

Founder Sharechat

Nirupama Rao

India's Ambassador USA, Srilanka and China

Nirupama Menon Rao is a retired Indian diplomat, Foreign Secretary and Ambassador. She was educated in India and joined the Indian Foreign Service in 1973. During her four-decade-long diplomatic career she held several important assignments. She was India’s first woman spokesperson in the Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi, the first woman high commissioner from her country to Sri Lanka, and the first Indian woman ambassador to the People’s Republic of China. She served as India’s Foreign Secretary from 2009-2011. At the end of that term, she was appointed India’s Ambassador to the United States where she served for a term of two years from 2011-2013.

Kunal Shah

CEO freecharge

Mr. Kunal Shah is a Co-Founder of Accelyst Solutions Pvt. Ltd. and served as its Chief Executive Officer until May 2016. He is a Founder of Freecharge. He serves as an Advisor at Sequoia Capital India. Mr. Shah has been the Chairman of the Board at Accelyst Solutions Pvt. Ltd. since May 2016.

Sandeep Aggarwal

Founder Shopclues

Sandeep is the founder of two marketplaces in India – Droom, India’s first automobile marketplace and ShopClues, India’s first and largest managed marketplace. Sandeep is an angel investor, serial entrepreneur, philanthropist and Internet visionary and has successfully managed to build ShopClues as one of the top 5 Unicorns in Consumer Internet in India. Having worked in Silicon Valley and Seattle for about 12 years prior to ShopClues & Droom, Sandeep has rich experience in the global Internet and E-commerce industry. In April 2014, Sandeep started Droom, an online transactional marketplace for buying and selling of used and new automobiles. Droom is a completely disruptive business which has created an unparalleled trust and pricing advantage for buyers. Prior to Droom in October 2010, Sandeep founded ShopClues.com at his house in California and moved to India in August 2011 to launch the brand as India’s first managed marketplace to empower 15Mn SME sellers in various unstructured categories such as fashion, household, electronic and many more.

VG Siddhartha

Founder Cafe Coffee Day

Mr. V. G. Siddhartha is the Founder and Owner of Coffee Day Enterprises Limited and has been its Chairman of the Board and Managing Director since January 17, 2015. Mr. Siddhartha founded Amalgamated Bean Coffee Trading Company Limited in 1992. He founded Coffee Day Trading Ltd. in 2000 and serves as its Managing Partner and Director. Mr. Siddhartha's responsibilities include strategizing value creation for shareholders and propel growth of return on investment and maintain investor relations. He is also the owner of Coffee Day Resorts Private Limited. He took over Sivan Securities Ltd. in 1984 and turned it into a highly successful investment banking and stock broking company. He introduced equity research division in 1984. His prior experience includes internship in trading on the Indian Stock Market under Mr. Mahendra Kampani of JM Financials. He serves as the Chairman of the Board of Directors of Amalgamated Bean Coffee Trading Company Limited and Coffee Day Hotels & Resorts Pvt. Ltd. He serves as the Chairman of Way2Wealth Securities Pvt. Ltd. He served as a Non-Executive Director of MindTree Limited since January 20, 2000 until March 9, 2018. He serves as Director of Liqwid Krystal, Ittiam Systems (P) Ltd. and Ittiam. He holds a Masters in Economics from Mangalore University.
