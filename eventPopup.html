How to maximize clicks for display advertising? An approach using reinforcement learning

In the infield of Marketing, one of the core challenges is that the business condition changes all the time. A winning campaign strategy can become unfavored over time, while an old strategy can gain new traction. So far in digital marketing and web analytics, A/B testing is the prevalent method of comparing digital campaigns, choosing the winning ad, and deciding targeting strategy. A/B testing is excellent when testing variations on similar solutions and having one or more metrics that are clear indicators of success or failure. However, when faced with a complex problem or working on future topics, A/B testing fails to deliver. Achieving long-term impact from experimentation is demanding and resource-intensive. As firms aspire and compete to be fully digital with a limited marketing budget and deadlines to realize the tangible $ impact, shifting from conventional test and target strategy to the next-gen approach is imperative. This session proposes state-of-the-art techniques and presents their applications in Digital marketing campaigns.
