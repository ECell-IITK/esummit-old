Pitch Ur Product is organized with an aim to provide participants with an opportunity that can transform their innovation - something as simple and small scale initiative as their summer project or a research endeavor - into real world entrepreneurial ideas. Here, we examine your ability to evaluate the market, customer needs, product usability. Your plan of action to safeguard the intellectual rights of your product (majorly by creating entry barrier) and your future research goals associated with the same product or plans to launch parallel products. The last year winners are: BlissTab Law Briefs Zero Kelvin.

Screening Round: Participating teams need to fill up the form detailing the abstract of their idea. Shortlisting for the next round will be done on the basis of the abstract. Final Round: Shortlisted participants have to give a presentation of their abstract at e-Summit '18 where they will be judged by our panel of judges. This shall take place on 25th August at IIT Kanpur. The participants will make a presentation (15 slides maximum, including introduction) summarizing their product and business plan. 3-5 minutes are reserved to answer the questions raised by the judges, if any.

A team of maximum 5 members is allowed. Pooling projects from different clubs and departments of your institute is permitted. All interpretations of the rules and decisions of the judging panel and organising team, in all matters related to Pitch-ur-Product, are final. The judging panel and organising team reserve the right, in their sole discretion, to disqualify any individual they find or believe that participants have: Tampered with the entry process or operation of the competition, Violated any of these rules, or Plagiarised the idea, in parts or as a whole Under all circumstances, the decision of the judging committee will be final.

The prize money comprises of 35K will be awarded to the winners as follows.
1st Prize: 20,000 INR
2nd Prize: 10,000 INR
3rd Prize: 5,000 INR

Get ready to compete with the sharpest and fastest minds with a passion for the business world.. The Business quiz will cover the entire gamut of the Business World – Personalities, Companies, Technologies, Innovations, Marketing, Financial Institutions, and Terminologies across different verticals – Media & Entertainment, Retail, Hospitality, Software, E-Commerce and other aspects of the global world. With quiz masters rapidly firing questions, only lightning fast responses and out of the box thinking can sail you through the tough competition.
In the First Round, there will be a written examination (Paper Based) details for which will be informed later. The Topic for quizzing event are current affairs, general knowledge and issues related to the corporate world.
Top 10 teams will face the challenges of our Quiz Master in the Finals of the quiz scheduled on the same day.
Each team will have a maximum of 3 members. The event will take place in two rounds, prelims followed by the finals. Approximately 10 teams from the prelims will qualify for the finals. In the finals, the quiz master will explain detailed rules before conducting every round. The quiz master’s decision is final and abiding.
